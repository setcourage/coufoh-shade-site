About Us
Coufoh Shade Limited is a multi-faceted company offering
expertise in construction, real estate development, general
contracting, facility management and consultancy.
We are known for our commitment to delivering high-quality
residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects while maintaining
excellence in general contracting and offering advisory services.
Our focus on innovation, integrity, and sustainability ensures that
we meet client needs with tailored solutions.
Our experienced team has extensive knowledge of the industry, and
we are dedicated to being a trusted name in these fields across
Nigeria and beyond.
